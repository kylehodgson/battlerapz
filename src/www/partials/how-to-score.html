Once the rappers names have been entered and the winner of the coin toss has been selected, you are ready to score.

One punch is given for:

  • wordplay - metaphors, acronyms, puns, etc.

  • content - reference to event, person, thing etc.

  • disrespect- insults, threats etc.

  • freestyle - lines invented on the spot.

  • boasting - praising oneself.

  • rebuttals - comeback to a line.

    • The more combined punches per line the higher the score of that line.

    Scoring examples:

  • calling your opponent fat would get 1 punch (personal attack)

  • calling your opponent fat by making use of wordplay would get 2 punches (personal attack + wordplay)

  • calling your opponent fat by making use of wordplay which also uses a sports reference would get 3 punches (personal attack + wordplay + content)

    At the end of a round the total of punches is shown.

    Performance is scored out of 5 stars for:

  • rapping - delivery in rhyme and rhythm.

  • jokes - making use of humour.

  • aggression - assertiveness or hostility.

  • delivery - composure and style in performance.

  • strategy - carrying out a plan.

  • schemes - using words around a theme.

  • antics - using props.

  • freshness - never used lines.

    • Negative Points are scored out of 5 stars for:

  • chokes - forgetting one's lines.

  • reaches - using words so that they lose their meaning.
